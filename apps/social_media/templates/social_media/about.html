KittenStagram: My "Pet Project"

General

KittenStagram is a silly, cat-themed take on a popular social media site. Its primary purpose is to serve as a portfolio example of my work as a web developer; its secondary purpose is to aide in one of the most important quests of the internet: to have all the cats.

Nuts and Bolts

KittenStagram is a mobile-first web application built using Django (a Python Web Framework) and Bootstrap v4 as its main components. Additional styling is provided by Isotope, Google Fonts, Cropper.js, Font Awesome, JSTZ, and the developers kind enough to share their wisdom in forums and blog posts. A very special thanks goes to Vitor Freitas for his blog simple is better than complex, which helped me create a more dynamic and entertaining site than I had originally imagined.
